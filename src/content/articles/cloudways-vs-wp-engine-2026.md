# Cloudways vs WP Engine 2026: Managed WordPress Hosting Showdown

Choosing between Cloudways and WP Engine for managed WordPress hosting? You're looking at two powerhouses with completely different approaches. After testing both platforms extensively for 6 months with real WordPress sites, we've uncovered the key differences that matter most.

WP Engine dominates the premium managed WordPress space with their proprietary EverCache technology and enterprise-grade features. Cloudways takes a different route, offering managed cloud hosting across multiple providers (AWS, Google Cloud, DigitalOcean) at significantly lower prices.

Both excel in their target markets, but the choice depends entirely on your priorities: performance at any cost versus flexibility and value.

## Quick Verdict

**Choose WP Engine if:** You need enterprise-grade WordPress hosting with premium support, advanced developer tools, and don't mind paying for the best performance. Sites handling 100,000+ monthly visitors will benefit most.

**Choose Cloudways if:** You want cloud hosting flexibility with managed convenience at competitive prices. Perfect for agencies managing multiple client sites or developers who need staging environments and server control.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is WP Engine worth the extra cost over Cloudways?**
A: WP Engine justifies its premium pricing for high-traffic WordPress sites requiring maximum performance and enterprise features. For most projects, Cloudways offers better value unless you specifically need WP Engine's WordPress optimizations and premium support.

**Q: Can I migrate from Cloudways to WP Engine or vice versa?**
A: Yes, both platforms offer migration services. WP Engine provides free automated migration for new customers, while Cloudways offers a WordPress migrator plugin and professional migration services for complex sites.

**Q: Which platform is better for WooCommerce stores?**
A: Cloudways edges out WP Engine for e-commerce due to better scaling options, multiple platform support, and more cost-effective resource allocation. However, WP Engine offers excellent WooCommerce optimization for high-traffic stores.

**Q: Do both platforms offer staging environments?**
A: Yes, both provide staging sites. WP Engine offers one-click staging with their plans, while Cloudways allows unlimited staging environments. For more staging options, see our [Best Web Hosting with Staging Environment 2026](/best-hosting-staging-environment-2026) guide.

**Q: Which has better customer support?**
A: WP Engine provides superior support with WordPress specialists available 24/7 via phone and chat. Cloudways offers good support but focuses more on platform-specific issues rather than WordPress expertise.

**Q: Can I use custom plugins with both platforms?**
A: Cloudways allows all WordPress plugins without restrictions. WP Engine has a list of prohibited plugins that conflict with their caching system, though most popular plugins work fine.
