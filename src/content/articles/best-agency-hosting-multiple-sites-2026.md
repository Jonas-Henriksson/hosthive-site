## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the difference between regular hosting and agency hosting?

Agency hosting includes multi-site management dashboards, white-label options, team collaboration tools, and billing integration that regular hosting lacks. You're paying for features that let you efficiently manage dozens or hundreds of client sites from centralized control panels, rather than logging into separate accounts for each site.

### How many client sites can I host on one account?

This varies dramatically by provider. Cloudways and Hostinger offer unlimited sites (limited by server resources), while Kinsta charges per site installation. SiteGround's reseller plans include 5-10 client accounts. For agencies managing 20+ sites, cloud platforms like Cloudways typically offer better value than per-site pricing.

### Do I need reseller hosting or managed hosting for my agency?

Reseller hosting (like SiteGround's plans) gives you white-label branding and client billing capabilities but requires more technical management. Managed hosting (like Kinsta or WP Engine) handles server maintenance and optimization but costs more per site. Choose based on your technical expertise and profit margins.

### What uptime should I promise clients?

Never promise higher uptime than your hosting provider guarantees. Most premium hosts offer 99.9% uptime SLAs, which translates to about 43 minutes of downtime per month. Our testing showed Cloudways and Kinsta consistently exceeding 99.98% uptime, making them safe choices for demanding clients.

### How important are staging environments for agency work?

Staging environments are essential for professional agency work. They let you test updates, design changes, and new features without risking client sites. Kinsta includes staging for every site, while Cloudways offers easy staging deployment. Avoid hosts that charge extra for this critical feature.

### Should I white-label my hosting or be transparent about the provider?

This depends on your business model. White-labeling lets you maintain complete control over the client relationship and potentially charge hosting markups. However, transparency about using premium providers like Kinsta or Cloudways can actually build trust and justify your rates. Many successful agencies are transparent about their hosting partnerships.

### What backup frequency do I need for client sites?

Daily automated backups are the minimum for professional agency work. Weekly backups are insufficient for active business sites. Look for hosts offering multiple restore points (14-30 days) and easy restore processes. Some clients may require hourly backups for mission-critical applications — factor this into your hosting choice and pricing.
