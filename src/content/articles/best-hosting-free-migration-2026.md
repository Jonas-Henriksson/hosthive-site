## How to Prepare for Website Migration

### Before Migration Checklist

1. **Create Full Backup**: Always backup your current site before migration
2. **Document Current Setup**: Note plugins, themes, and custom configurations
3. **Check File Sizes**: Large sites may require special handling
4. **Verify Email Settings**: Plan for email service continuity
5. **Update Software**: Ensure WordPress, plugins, and themes are current
6. **Test Staging**: If available, test migration on staging environment first

### During Migration Best Practices

- **Communicate Timeline**: Inform users of potential downtime windows
- **Monitor Progress**: Stay in contact with migration team
- **Test Functionality**: Verify all features work post-migration
- **Update DNS Gradually**: Consider gradual DNS changes for large sites
- **Check Performance**: Monitor site speed and uptime

### Post-Migration Tasks

1. **Verify All Content**: Check pages, posts, and media files
2. **Test Contact Forms**: Ensure forms and email delivery work
3. **Update URLs**: Fix any absolute URLs pointing to old host
4. **Monitor Analytics**: Ensure tracking codes function properly
5. **Check SEO Settings**: Verify redirects and XML sitemaps
6. **Test E-commerce**: For stores, test checkout and payment processing

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does free website migration take?

Migration times vary by provider and site complexity. Simple WordPress sites typically migrate within 24 hours, while complex e-commerce sites may take 48-72 hours. SiteGround offers the fastest migrations at 4-24 hours, while budget providers like Hostinger may take up to 72 hours.

### Will my website experience downtime during migration?

Premium providers like SiteGround, Kinsta, and WP Engine use staging environments to ensure zero downtime migrations. Budget hosts may require 1-4 hours of downtime. Always discuss downtime expectations with your chosen provider before starting migration.

### How many websites can I migrate for free?

This varies significantly by provider. WP Engine and Kinsta offer unlimited free migrations, SiteGround includes one free migration, Bluehost provides up to 5 free migrations, while Hostinger and Cloudways typically include one free migration per account or server.

### What if my migration fails or has issues?

All reputable hosts provide post-migration support to resolve issues. Most offer 30-day support periods and will re-attempt migrations if problems occur. Premium providers like SiteGround and Kinsta have 99%+ success rates and dedicated support teams to handle complex situations.

### Can I migrate from any hosting provider?

Most migration services can transfer sites from any cPanel-based host. However, some limitations exist for proprietary hosting platforms or very old server configurations. Contact your target host's support team to confirm compatibility before purchasing hosting.

### Do I need to backup my site before migration?

Yes, always create a full backup before migration, even when using professional migration services. While migration success rates are high, having your own backup ensures you can restore your site if unexpected issues occur during the transfer process.
