## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes WordPress hosting different from regular web hosting?
WordPress hosting is specifically optimized for WordPress sites with features like one-click WordPress installation, automatic updates, WordPress-specific caching, staging environments, and expert WordPress support. Regular hosting may require manual WordPress setup and optimization.

### How much should I expect to pay for quality WordPress hosting?
Quality WordPress hosting ranges from 2.99/month (budget shared hosting) to 35.00/month+ (premium managed hosting). Most users find excellent value in the 5-15/month range, which typically includes managed features, good performance, and reliable support.

### Do I need managed WordPress hosting or is shared hosting sufficient?
Shared hosting works for small sites with basic needs, while managed WordPress hosting is better for business sites, high-traffic blogs, or users who want hands-off maintenance. Managed hosting includes automatic updates, enhanced security, staging sites, and expert WordPress support.

### Can I migrate my existing WordPress site to a new host?
Yes, all major WordPress hosts offer migration assistance. Premium providers like WP Engine, Kinsta, and SiteGround provide free expert migrations, while budget hosts typically offer migration plugins or tools. Most migrations complete within 24-48 hours with minimal downtime.

### How important is server location for WordPress hosting?
Server location significantly impacts site speed, especially for regional audiences. Choose hosting with data centers close to your primary audience. Providers like Kinsta and Cloudways offer multiple global locations, while others may be limited to specific regions.

### What's the difference between WordPress.com and WordPress.org hosting?
WordPress.com is a hosted platform with limited customization, while WordPress.org hosting (what we review here) gives you full control over your WordPress site. WordPress.org hosting requires a separate hosting provider but offers complete flexibility with themes, plugins, and customizations.
